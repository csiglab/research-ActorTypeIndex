Planetary Actors

Mapping the abstract space of social actors at the planetary scale. Identifying the most relevant entities across multiple classifications.

Abstract

In this project we attempt to represent the abstract space of social actors at the planetary scale. The objective is to identify the most relevant actors across multiple categories.

Goals

  • Create a modeling template for each actor type.
  • Represent individual actors by applying the template.
