Strategic Description

TechNova Industries is a global industrial technology corporation specializing in advanced manufacturing systems, automation solutions, and industrial IoT platforms. The firm operates across three primary business segments: Industrial Automation (45% of revenue), Smart Manufacturing Systems (35%), and Industrial Software & Services (20%).

With headquarters in Stuttgart, Germany, TechNova maintains operational presence across 18 countries with primary manufacturing facilities in Germany, China, and the United States. The firm is recognized as an industry leader in Industry 4.0 solutions, serving automotive, aerospace, and general manufacturing sectors.
