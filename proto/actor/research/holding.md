# Research Holding Representation Model

> A macro-level framework for mapping the strategy, governance, and aggregate value of a network of affiliated research entities.

## 1. Holding Mandate & Strategy (The "Meta-Why")

* **Portfolio Thesis:** The overarching goal uniting the subsidiaries (e.g., deep tech commercialization, national security, fundamental science).
* **Holding Model:** Financial holding (hands-off, ROI focused) vs. Strategic holding (hands-on, synergy focused).
* **Corporate Governance:** Board of Directors, Holding-level scientific advisory, and executive management.

## 2. Central Resources & Capital (The "Macro-Inputs")

* **Capital Allocation:** How the central fund distributes budget, grants, or venture capital among child entities.
* **Centralized Shared Services:** Consolidated Legal/IP departments, centralized HR, and enterprise IT.
* **Strategic Infrastructure:** Macro-assets shared across the network (e.g., a centralized supercomputing cluster, real estate portfolios, shared testing grounds).
* **Executive Talent:** Recruitment of Institute Directors, subsidiary CEOs, and high-level Principal Investigators (PIs).

## 3. Portfolio Structure (The "Entities")

* **Subsidiary Typology:** Wholly-owned labs, joint ventures, public-private partnerships, or academic affiliates.
* **Domain Distribution:** How research domains are divided or deliberately overlapped across the different child organizations.
* **Lifecycle Management:** Incubation of new institutes, scaling of successful ones, and divesting/closing underperforming ones.

## 4. Coordination & Governance (The "Glue")

* **Autonomy vs. Control:** The degree of independence granted to child organizations regarding their own research agendas.
* **Cross-Pollination:** Mechanisms to force or incentivize collaboration between sibling entities (e.g., joint-institute grants, shared fellowships).
* **Performance Oversight:** How the holding evaluates the success of a child entity (KPI dashboards, periodic external audits, ROI tracking).

## 5. Aggregate Output & Value Capture (The "Meta-Output")

* **IP Pooling & Licensing:** Centralized patent portfolios and large-scale technology transfer operations.
* **Ecosystem Creation:** Spin-off networks, centralized startup incubators/accelerators, and venture arms.
* **Aggregate Impact:** Combined societal influence, policy shaping, and macro-level reputation of the holding brand.
