# Research Organization Representation Model

> A systemic framework for mapping the structure, operations, and impact of a research entity.

## 1. Identity & Strategy (The "Why")

* **Mission and Vision:** Core purpose and long-term scientific goals
* **Research Domains:** Primary fields of study and specialized focus areas
* **Governance:** Board of directors, scientific advisory boards, and leadership structure
* **Culture:** Academic vs. corporate, open science vs. proprietary

## 2. Resources & Inputs (The "What we need")

* **Finance:** Funding sources (grants, private equity, endowments), budgeting, and financial allocation
* **Infrastructure:** Physical labs, specialized equipment, computing power, and facilities
* **Talent (Personnel):** Researchers, principal investigators (PIs), postdocs, and administrative support

## 3. Internal Structure & Operations (The "How we work")

* **Organizational Matrix:** Departments, individual labs, cross-functional teams, and support staff
* **Project Management:** Grant administration, research timelines, and milestone tracking
* **Ethics & Compliance:** Institutional Review Boards (IRB), data privacy, and regulatory adherence
* **Partnerships:** Academic networks, industry collaborations, and joint research initiatives

## 4. Outputs & Impact (The "What we produce")

* **Academic Output:** Published papers, preprints, conference proceedings, and datasets
* **Commercial Output:** Patents, intellectual property (IP), and technology transfer
* **Entrepreneurial Output:** Spin-offs, startup incubation, and commercial licensing
* **Impact Metrics:** Citation counts, societal impact, and institutional reputation

## 5. Evolution & Dynamics (The "How we grow")

* **Knowledge Accumulation:** Institutional memory,
internal databases, and expertise development
* **Career Progression:** Recruitment pipelines, researcher training, and tenure tracks
* **Strategic Adaptability:** How the organization pivots in response to new technologies (e.g., AI integration) or shifting funding landscapes
